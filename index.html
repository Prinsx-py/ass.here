".ass" stands for Advanced SubStation Alpha — a plain-text subtitle format for advanced timed text. It supports styling, precise timing (including per-syllable/karaoke-style timing), positioning, and rich formatting used in captions and fansubbing.

Use cases: captioning videos, timed lyrics, karaoke, fansubbing, and sharing styled subtitles with timestamps. Uploads here let others download and reuse .ass files across players and projects.
